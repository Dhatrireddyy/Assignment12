\frametitle{Solving a}

$h\brak{0^{-}} = h\brak{0^{+}} = 0$\\
$h\brak{t} = \dfrac{1}{3}\brak{e^{-2t} - e^{-5t}}U\brak{t}$ and with $q\brak{t} = 5U\brak{t}$
